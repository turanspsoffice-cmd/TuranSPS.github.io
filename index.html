SAIRME Mineral Waters

Sairme Mineral Waters, in Georgia's Imereti region, are waters that form in the deep layers of the earth, naturally enriched with carbon dioxide, and have a balanced composition with beneficial minerals like calcium, magnesium, sodium. Sairme water, rich with natural minerals and lightly carbonated, is an ideal choice for both daily drinking and a healthy lifestyle. About Turan SPS

Turan SPS is a leading company founded in 2024 and operating in the field of distribution of quality beverages and water products in Azerbaijan. As the official distributor of Georgia's Boom Group company, our company has exclusive sales rights for the most popular and beloved Chito Gvrito lemonades in our country. In addition, Turan SPS collaborates with the manufacturer of Georgia's famous Sairme therapeutic waters and is the only official distributor of this water in Azerbaijan. Sairme waters, rich with beneficial minerals for the organism, providing natural freshness, are presented to our customers in premium quality. Our company is also the official distributor of premium fruit juices from IRIB Group located in the Dagestan Autonomous Republic of Russia. IRIB juices are distinguished by natural fruit taste and high quality standards, presented to our customers in Azerbaijan thanks to Turan SPS. As Turan SPS, our goal is not only to provide products to our customers, but also to ensure natural taste, freshness and health in every sip. By bringing together the highest quality lemonades, fruit juices, therapeutic and natural spring waters in Azerbaijan, we strive to make every day more delicious and refreshing.
